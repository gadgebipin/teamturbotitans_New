Team Turbo Titans from Amrutvahini College of Engineering is an enthusiastic and dedicated group of engineering students passionately participating in the REEV (Range-Extended Electric Vehicle) competition organized by SAE India.

Our Achievements:

Congratulations on achieving the second rank in the SAE India REEV (virtuals) competition! This remarkable accomplishment showcases our team's dedication, expertise, and innovative approach in the field of Hybrid electric vehicle engineering. Placing second among teams from across India emphasizes our commitment to excellence and our relentless pursuit of advancing sustainable mobility solutions. This achievement is a testament to our hard work, technical proficiency, and the collaborative spirit that drives us toward redefining the future of electric vehicles.
